Common questions
FAQs
What is the due date for GSTR-3B? +
For most registered taxpayers, GSTR-3B is due on the 20th of the following month. Quarterly filers under the QRMP scheme file by the 22nd or 24th, depending on the state. RK Consultancy sends due-date reminders to all clients in advance.
Is GST registration mandatory for my business? +
GST registration is mandatory if your aggregate turnover exceeds ₹40 lakhs for goods (₹20 lakhs for services). Special category states have lower thresholds. E-commerce operators must register regardless of turnover. We advise on threshold applicability for your specific case.
Can you respond to a GST notice on my behalf? +
Yes. We prepare detailed replies to show-cause notices, gather supporting documents, and attend department hearings where required. Our team has handled demand orders, audit notices and SCNs under GST.
How fast is ITR filing once I submit documents? +
Once all documents are received — Form 16, bank statements, investment proofs — we prepare and file within 24 to 48 hours. Refunds are processed by the Income Tax department directly to your linked bank account.
Do you serve clients outside Tamil Nadu? +
Yes, RK Consultancy serves businesses and individuals across India. All work is handled digitally — document exchange via WhatsApp or email, portal filing using your credentials, and e-signing via DSC or Aadhaar OTP.
